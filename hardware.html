Alius 6502 - Hardware

Chips, Parts, PCBs

Overview of hardware

The Alius 6502 is based on the Western Design Center WDC65C02 CPU. Other support chips are based around what would have been available in around 1980. The I/O is based on two 65C22 VIA chips. The keypad is just a row and column arrangement of push switches. The display is six 7-segment modules driven from six shift registers.

All parts come to a total cost of around $120USD
