Alius 6502 - Overview

A simple kitset computer based on the 6502 CPU

Welcome to Alius 6502

The Alius 6502 is a DIY educational computer kit that anyone can build, no matter your experience level. Whether you’re new to computers or already tech-savvy, Alius 6502 is designed to help you learn more about how computers work.

What makes Alius 6502 special?

It’s completely open source. Every part of Alius 6502 from the hardware to the software and the documentation is freely available for you to explore, modify, and share. Everything you need to know is published for free and then you can assemble the computer yourself while learning how each component works along the way.

The Alius 6502 isn’t just about building a computer, it’s about understanding it. Through the project you will learn the nuts and bolts of computing in a fun, hands-on way.

Whether you're looking to expand your skills or just curious about how computers work, the Alius 6502 is here to teach you.
